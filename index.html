The story of our barangay's name, "Bullogan," comes from the root word "bullog," which means that people in the old times would come together or gather. They would carry the deceased to the church because there were no roads back then, only paths. So, they would carry the coffin to the church. The name Bullogan comes from the fact that while they were carrying the coffin together, they would also carry guns, which is why our barangay is called Bullogan.

BARANGAY BULLOGAN IS COMPOSED OF 8 PUROKS

PUROK TISA
PUROK SAMBAG
PUROK TALISAY
PUROK IBA
PUROK NANGKA
PUROK TAMBIS
PUROK MANGGA
PUROK LAGUNDI
