This page is introducing our beautiful barangay and Purok

Bullogan is a barangay in the municipality of Dumanjug, in the province of Cebu. Its population as determined by the 2020 Census was 1,031. This represented 1.78% of the total population of Dumanjug.

Location

Bullogan is situated in Dumanjug, Cebu, approximately at coordinates 10.0102, 123.4690, on the island of Cebu.
